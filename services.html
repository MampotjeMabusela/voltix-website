01 — Solar

Solar Solutions

Purpose: Turn sunlight into dependable electricity for homes, estates, and businesses.

What we do: Custom system design, professional installation, grid-tie or hybrid setups, and compliance certification.

Client benefit: Lower energy bills, reduced carbon footprint, and long-term savings with premium hardware warranties.

02 — Backup

Backup Power Systems

Purpose: Keep essential loads running when the grid fails or load-shedding strikes.

What we do: Supply and install inverters, battery banks, and automatic changeover systems sized to your property.

Client benefit: Uninterrupted power, protected appliances, and total peace of mind during outages.

03 — Electrical

Electrical Installations

Purpose: Build safe, code-compliant electrical infrastructure that supports modern energy systems.

What we do: DB boards, circuit breakers, wiring, earthing, and full electrical fit-outs for new builds and upgrades.

Client benefit: Certified workmanship, improved safety, and a foundation ready for solar, backup, and EV integration.

04 — EV

EV Charging Stations

Purpose: Future-proof properties with convenient, scalable electric vehicle charging.

What we do: Site surveys, AC/DC charger installation, load management, and optional billing integration for estates and commercial sites.

Client benefit: Added property value, resident convenience, and optional revenue from shared charging infrastructure.

05 — Maintenance

Maintenance & Support

Purpose: Protect your investment with ongoing care that keeps systems performing at their peak.

What we do: Scheduled inspections, preventative maintenance, fault finding, repairs, and rapid technical support.

Client benefit: Fewer failures, extended equipment life, and expert help whenever you need it.

06 — Monitoring

Smart Monitoring

Purpose: Give you full visibility over production, storage, grid use, and system health in real time.

What we do: Configure monitoring platforms, dashboards, alerts, and performance reporting for solar and backup systems.

Client benefit: Data-driven savings, early fault detection, and confidence that everything is running as it should.
